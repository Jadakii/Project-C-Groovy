Shouldn't - Be
Luke Chiang
The song "Shouldn't Be" is by Luke Chiang, an R&B/soul artist from the United States. He is known for his emotive vocals and thoughtful production. The song "Shouldn't Be" gained significant popularity on TikTok, with over 140,000 videos using it. It was written after a breakup and explores themes of love and loss.

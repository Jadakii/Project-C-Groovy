Red Sneakers
IU
"Red Sneakers" is a cheerful, refreshing song by IU, part of her album "A Flower Bookmark 3". It features IU's signature cute and youthful voice and is described as a track that blends music and poetry, according to IU's social media. The song lyrics describe a couple enjoying a romantic night under the light of the "strawberry moon".
